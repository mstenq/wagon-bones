# Bosses
Bosses are more or less the same as in Balatro. Since you can't turn dice over to not know what it is, instead we're going with dirty/grimey dice that you don't know what its enhancements/aura/pips are like till you select dice to roll. Also there are no suit disabling so instead there is an odd/even disable boss.

- The Inspector - exhaust 2 random dice from your hand after each scoring round (move to spent pile)
- The Tax Man - playing your most played hand sets money to zero
- The Swamp - first 10 dice are covered in grime (unknown)
- The Marathon - distance is 4x normal
- The Broken Wagon Wheel - 1 in 7 dice are covered in grime (unknown)
- The Ghost Town - all played even pips are disabled
- The Undertaker - all played odd pips are disabled
- The Preacher - only one hand type allowed for round
- The Call Girl - must play different hands each round
- The Trickster - every played hand reduces trail knowledge by one before scoring
- The River - only straights count when scoring
- The Dust Storm - dice drawn after playing are covered in grime (unknown)
- The Sheriff - only 1 re-roll per day
- The Chain Gang - Start with -1 hand size
- The Standoff - Play only 1 hand
- The Banker - Lose $1 per dice scored
- The Bottle - Base trail knowledge is halved for entire round
- The Jinx - one random piece of equipment disabled per day
- The Bank Lien - all dice disabled until one piece of equipment sold
- The Bounty - disables re-rolling of one random dice per day
- The Land Slide - Flips and Shuffles all Equipment (Can't see front of card)
