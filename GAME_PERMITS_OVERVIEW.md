## Frontier Permits Overview

Frontier Permit (What balatro would call a voucher) show up in the shop, 1 per leg of journey(So 8 total) and give you bonuses to adjust how the game is played and can be very powerful. Each permit has 2 stages, and you can't see the second permit unless you've already purchased the first one.

## Frontier Permits
Supply Wagon - stage 1 - +1 card slot available in shop (3 slots total)
Freight Caravan - stage 2 - +1 additional card slot available in shop (4 slots total)

Bargain Bin - stage 1 - All cards/dice/packs/vouchers are 25% off
Estate Auction - stage 2 - All cards/dice/packs/vouchers are 50% off

Spirit Ritual - stage 1 - Ghost/Holy/Fire/Ice aura is 2x more likely to appear
Sacred Ceremony - stage 2 - Ghost/Holy/Fire/Ice aura is 4X more likely to appear

Lucky Streak - stage 1 - Rerolls cost $2 less
Devil's Luck - stage 2 - Rerolls cost an additional $2 less

Devil's Eye - stage 1 - +1 consumable slot
Infernal Vision - stage 2 - Frontier Experience cards may appear in supply packs  (20% chance that supply card is replaced with frontier exp card)

Binoculars - stage 1 - Trail guides packs always contain the trail guide for your most played hand
Surveryor's Scope - stage 2 - Trail guide cards in your consumable area give x1.5 mult for their specified hand when played

Extra Rations - stage 1 - Permanently gain +1 day per round
Supply Cache - stage 2 - Permanently gain an additional +1 day per round

Second Chance - stage 1 - Permanently gain _+1 reroll per round
Third Time's Charm - stage 2 - Permanently gain an additional +1 reroll per round

Camp Merchant - stage 1 - Supply cards appear 2x more frequently in the shop
Supply Baron - stage 2 - Supply cards appear 4x more frequently in the shop

Trail Cartographer - stage 1 - Trail guide cards appear 2x more frequently in the shop
Frontier Pathfinder - stage 2 - Trail guide cards appear 4x more frequently in the shop

Savings Bond - Raise the cap on interest earned each round to $10
Railroad Investment - Raise the cap on interest earned each round to $20

Strange Coin - stage 1 - Does nothing?
Bottomless Satchel - stage 2 - +1 equipment slot

Dice Carver - Enhanced Dice can be purchased from the shop
Master Engraver - Enhanced Dice with stickers may appear in the shop

Shortcut Trail - stage 1 - reduce required score by one leg, -1 day of travel per round
Hidden Pass - stage 2 - reduce required score by another leg, -1 reroll per round

Bounty Board - stage 1 - Reroll boss 1 timer per leg for $10
Wanted Dead or Alive - stage 2 - Reroll Boss unlimited times, $10 per roll

Supply Pouch - stage 1 - +1 hand size
Pack Mule - stage 2 - +1 hand size again
