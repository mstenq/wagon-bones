# Held in hand dice and items
Steel dice held in hand will do x1.5mult per trigger. Red bullet sticker will cause it to retrigger.
Gold dice held in hand will give $3 dollars at end of round.
Blue moon stickers held in hand at end of round will give a trail guide for the hand played last.
