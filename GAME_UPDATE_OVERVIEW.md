## Potential Update to make it more like cubito

The biggest change is removing hand types from the game and instead having dice that show blank/bullet/coin/foot/star.

Breakdown of dice symbols

- blanks: do nothing - rolling all blanks ends your day without any scoring
- bullet: gain +2 mult - chance to activate special conditions on items cards
- coin: gain $3 - possibility to activate special conditions on item cards
- foot: gain +10 miles - chance to activate special conditions on items cards
- star: gain a supply card - chance to activate special ability

You get a starting hand size of 10 dice. You still pick the dice you want to roll and they get spent if they score. Blanks dice stay active. You can pay to refresh your dice, the cost is however many dice are in your active colleciton. So the more dice you use the cheaper it gets to refresh untill you've used all of them and then you get your dice refreshed automatically. 

You can reroll as many times as you want, but if you get reroll and the new dice all come up as blanks that day ends with zero score/rewards. If you bust none of your dice are moved to the spent pile.

# Dice Info
- Standard Dice - 1 coin, 1 bullet, 4 blanks
- Bone Dice - 
- Lucky Dice + chance at money and mult
- Wooden Dice + Add 10 base miles
- Iron Dice + x1.5 mult when held in hand
- Gold Dice + Add money when kept
- Diamond Dice + x2mult and 25% chance of cracking
- Stone Dice + no pips, counts as 50 base miles
