# Equipment Overview
Equipment/Items take the place of jokers. Though we will just show them as cards in the game.

## Equipment Aura 
Equipment can also have Aura with the same affects as the dice aura, except for the addition of ghost aura which is essentially a like a negative joker in Balatro.

- holy aura - x1.5 mult
- fire -  +10 mult
- icy - + 50 miles
- ghost - doesn't take up equipment slot
