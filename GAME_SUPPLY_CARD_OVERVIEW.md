# Supply Card Overview
This is like Balatro's tarot cards. But one major difference is that we don't enhance existing dice, you always add new dice to your pouch. This is because we start with a relatively small collection of dice, so adding special dice and remove crappy dice is the only way to fix your dice collection. Otherwise it would be way too easy. 


- coffee tin - create 2 iron dice
- buzzards - create 2 bone dice
- rabbits foot - create 2 lucky dice
- firewood - create 2 wooden dice
- loaded - create one loaded dice (Select a number 1-6)
- pick axe - create one diamond dice
- pan for gold - create 1 golden dice
- treasure map - double your money (max of $20)
- shallow grave - select 5 random dice from your active dice collection and choose 2 to destory (Does not target spent dice)
- trade - get money equal to value of equipment (Max of $50)
- doctor -  creates 2 medicine cards in your inventory (Must have room)
   - medicine cards can only be gained from a doctor card in packs, they can bump a dice value up or down by 1 value
- compass - get 2 random trail guides in your inventory (Must have room)
- supply cache - get 2 random supply cards in your inventory (Must have room)
- ingenuity - get 1 random piece of equipment (Must have room)
- mirage - pick 5 random dice from your available dice, select two. The left one turns into the right one. (Does not target spent dice)
- bless - 1 in 4 chance to randomly bless a piece of equipment with aura (holy/fire/ice)
- second helpings - creates the last used supply or trail guide used and puts it in your inventory (Must have room)
