## TRAIL EVENTS (Between-Round Narrative Moments)

**The Big Idea:** After beating a leg but before the shop, a random trail event occurs. These are choose-your-own-adventure moments with risk/reward.

---

### Frequency & Balance

**Every round before the shop, but with a weighted pool that includes "Uneventful Travel" as a possible outcome (~20% weight).** This means effectively ~80% of rounds have a real event, but the player always sees the event screen — so it doesn't feel like a missing feature when nothing happens, it feels like they got lucky. "Clear skies and smooth trail ahead" is its own little relief moment.

**Harm vs. Help ratio:**

Target split: **~45% negative / ~30% choice (risk/reward) / ~25% positive**

This mirrors the Oregon Trail feel where the trail is *hard* but punctuated by moments of fortune. Pure positive events feel cheap if they're too common, but they need to exist so players don't dread every event screen.

**Mitigation tools that already exist in the game:**
- **Money** — Many negative events offer a "pay to avoid" option, so being flush with cash is itself mitigation
- **Medicine cards** (from Doctor supply card / Dr. Eleanor Sykes) — Could be spent to negate sickness/injury events
- **Equipment sacrifice** — Several events let you trade equipment to avoid the penalty (thematic: burning supplies to survive)
- **Rerolls** — Some events cost future rerolls, so professions with extra rerolls (Farmer, Con Artist) are naturally resilient
- **Supply cards in inventory** — Having spare supply cards could provide "emergency" options on certain events
- **Specific professions** — Scout Caleb could get a "skip this event" option once per run; Hunter Nathan could auto-resolve animal events favorably

**New mitigation equipment:**
- **"Spare Wagon Parts"** ($5) — +4 mult. Automatically negates one wagon-damage event per run (broken wheel/axle/tongue), then destroys itself
- **"Scout's Spyglass"** ($6) — +25 miles. See the next trail event before it happens; choose to skip it for $3
- **"Providence"** (Legendary) — All negative effects from trail events are prevented. You still see the event, but divine favor intervenes on your behalf and you only reap the benefits.

### Examples:

- **"Abandoned Wagon"** — Gain 3 random enhanced dice, but next boss is upgraded (multiply base score by additional 1.5x)
- **"Animal Caught in Quicksand"** — Lose 1 random enhanced die, OR lose 2 rerolls next round to rescue it safely
- **"Animal Injured Stepping in a Hole"** — Lose 1 day next round (animal slows the group) OR use a medicine card to heal it instantly
- **"Animals Exhausted"** — -1 hand size next round OR pay $5 to rest them
- **"Bad Mosquitos"** — Lose 1 reroll next round (distracted animals)
- **"Bad Water"** — Lose $4 to buy clean water from a merchant, OR take the hit: -2 rerolls next round from sickness
- **"Bandit Ambush"** — Lose half your money OR sacrifice an equipment to fight them off (gain $10 bounty)
- **"Blizzard"** — Lose 1 day next round AND all dice scoring is reduced by 10 miles flat, OR pay $10 to hunker down (no penalty)
- **"Broken Wagon Axle"** — Lose 2 days next round OR pay $12 to replace it; if you have 'Spare Wagon Parts' equipment, free fix
- **"Broken Wagon Tongue"** — Lose 1 day next round OR pay $6 to fix; if you have 'Spare Wagon Parts' equipment, free fix
- **"Broken Wagon Wheel"** — Lose 1 day next round OR pay $8 to fix immediately; if you have 'Spare Wagon Parts' equipment, free fix
- **"Broken Yoke"** — -1 hand size next round (can't haul full load) OR pay $7 to replace; if you have 'Spare Wagon Parts' equipment, free fix
- **"Buffalo Stampede"** — 50% chance: gain 2 bone dice (you harvest bones). 50% chance: lose 1 equipment of your choice (trampled)
- **"Caught Some Fish"** — Gain $4 (sold extra to other travelers)
- **"Dead Livestock"** — Permanently lose 1 random enhanced die (represents lost hauling capacity)
- **"Dust Storm"** — -1 reroll per day next round (can't see clearly to aim rerolls)
- **"Extreme Cold"** — All diamond dice have doubled odds of cracking next round, OR pay $5 for firewood and gain a wood dice
- **"Extreme Heat"** — Lose $3 per day next round to water costs, OR sacrifice 2 random enhanced dice (animals drank your water supply)
- **"Fallen Rocks"** — 30% chance lose a random enhanced die, 70% gain a stone dice. No choice — pure bad luck moment
- **"Fallen Timbers"** — Pay $4 to clear the path, OR lose 1 day next round cutting through and gain 2 wooden dice
- **"Fellow Traveler"** — A stranger offers to trade: one of your equipment for a random uncommon equipment (sight unseen), yes or no? - random uncommon equipment will have ghost aura 50% of the time
- **"Fire in a Wagon"** — Lose all supply/trail guide cards from your inventory, OR try to save the supplies. 50% chance of losing 2 days next turn to injury, 50% chance to gain a wooden dice with fire aura
- **"Flooded Trail"** — Pay $5 to hire a ferry, OR risk it: 60% fine, 40% lose 1 random enhanced die
- **"Found Fruit"** — Gain 1 random supply card (free provisions)
- **"Gold Strike!"** — Lose 2 rerolls next leg to gain $10 and a gold die with holy aura
- **"Hailstorm"** — wait it out and lose 1 day next turn, or chance it. 50% chance to lose an enhanced dice, 50% chance to gain 1 stone dice with ice aura
- **"Heavy Fog"** — Next round you are unable to reroll on the first day (can't see where you're going), remaining days are normal
- **"Locusts"** — Lose all supply cards in your inventory (eaten), OR sacrifice 1 equipment to protect them
- **"Lose Trail"** — Lose 1 day next round (wandering)
- **"Lost Trail"** — Choose: lose 1 day next round, OR pay $4 for a guide
- **"Lost!"** — Lose 2 days next round (severe) OR pay $8 for rescue from a passing wagon train and gain "Bless" supply card
- **"Missing Livestock"** — Lose 2 random enhanced dice from collection (represent lost pack animals), OR pay $6 to search and recover them
- **"Missing Person"** — Press on: person is presumed dead. Search: spend $5 and 1 day next round,50% chance of finding them and a rare equipment card, 50% chance of gaining bone die (They're found dead).
- **"Native Guide"** — Offers 3 free trail guides of your choice, but you must skip the next shop
- **"No Grass"** — -1 day next round (animals starving, move slower) OR pay $4 for feed
- **"No Water"** — Lose $6 buying water at premium, OR take penalty: -2 rerolls next round
- **"Prairie Fire"** — Lose 1 random equipment OR pay $8 to drive through quickly (gain lucky dice with flaming aura)
- **"Quicksand"** — Lose 3 random enhanced dice from collection, OR sacrifice 1 equipment card of your choice to pull free
- **"Rough Trail"** — Next rounds base score is multiplied by x1.5
- **"River Crossing"** — Pay $5 to ford safely, or risk it: 50% chance nothing happens, 30% lose a random enhanced die, 20% find a rare equipment
- **"Severe Thunderstorm"** — All "lucky" type dice have their odds halved next round (bad luck vibes), OR use a medicine card to calm the animals
- **"Sick Oxen"** — Lose $3 per day next round, OR sacrifice a die to cure them
- **"Snowbound"** — Lose 2 days next round (trapped) OR pay $10 to dig out
- **"Spoiled Food"** — Lose 1 random supply card from inventory
- **"Strangers Ahead"** — Choice: Avoid them (nothing happens) or approach: 50% they're traders (free equipment reroll in next shop), 50% they're hostile (lose $5)
- **"Strangers Approach"** — They offer a gamble: pay $5, flip a coin — heads you get a gold die and $10 back, tails you lose your $5
- **"Swamped Wagon"** — Lose 2 random supply/trail guide cards OR pay $7 to salvage
- **"Theft From a Wagon"** — Lose random piece of equipment, OR if you have 22 Rifle or similar "weapon" equipment, scare them off (keep everything, gain $3)
- **"Thick Dust From Other Wagons"** — -1 reroll next round (minor nuisance, no choice)
- **"Thief!"** — Lose $8 OR sacrifice an enhanced die to chase them down (recover $8 + gain $4 bounty)
- **"Tipped Wagon"** — Lose 1 random equipment and 1 random die, OR pay $10 to right it (keep everything)
- **"Too Many People At the River"** — Lose 1 day next round waiting, OR pay $6 to cut in line
- **"Wagon Fell Through Ice"** — Lose 3 random enhanced dice AND 1 random equipment, OR pay $15 to hire help pulling it out
- **"Wagon Stuck in Deep Sand"** — Lose 1 day next round, OR sacrifice 2 enhanced dice to lighten the load (no day loss)
- **"Wagon Stuck in the Mud"** — Lose 1 day next round OR pay $5 for oxen team help
- **"Wagon Train Morale Is Low"** — Must use standard dice (non-enhanced dice) on first day next round, OR spend $5 on whiskey to boost morale (no penalty)
- **"Wild Fruit"** — Gain $3 and gain 1 random supply card
- **"Wild Vegetables"** — Gain 1 medicine card
- **"Wrong Trail"** — Lose 1 day next round AND next boss base miles requirement is multiplied by 1.1x 
- **"Abandoned Mine"** — Explore the shaft: 50% chance to gain 2 steel dice, 50% chance of cave-in (lose 1 day next round and 1 random die). Skip: nothing happens.
- **"Traveling Blacksmith"** — Pay $6 and sacrifice 2 wood or bone dice to forge a steel die with fire aura. If you have 'Spare Wagon Parts', the forge is free.
- **"Crystal Cave"** — Lose 1 day next round to explore, gain 1 diamond die with ice aura. Or skip and gain nothing.
- **"Old Prospector's Cache"** — A dying prospector gives you his stash. Gain 1 diamond die. If you give him a medicine card instead, gain a diamond die with holy aura.
- **"Iron Vein"** — Spend $4 and 2 wood dice to mine it — gain 2 steel dice. Or spend $8 (no dice cost) to hire miners — gain 1 steel die with ice aura, or skip.
- **"Sunken Chest"** — Found at a river crossing. Pay $5 to fish it out: 40% gain a gold die with fire aura, 30% gain 2 gold dice, 30% it's empty (lose your $5)
- **"Old Burial Ground"** — Respectfully pass through: gain 2 bone dice with holy aura. Disturb the graves: gain 4 bone dice, but lose 2 rerolls next round (cursed).
- **"Whale Bones"** — Massive skeleton half-buried in desert sand. Harvest them: gain 3 bone dice. Spend $4 to carve them: gain 1 bone die with fire aura and 1 bone die with ice aura.
- **"Four-Leaf Clover Patch"** — Gain 1 lucky die. Spend time searching the whole field (lose 1 day next round): gain 2 lucky dice with holy aura.
- **"Rabbit Warren"** — Catch rabbits for dinner: gain $3 and 1 lucky die. Set elaborate traps (pay $4): gain 2 lucky dice, one with a red bullet pip enhancement.
- **"Shooting Star"** — Make a wish. 50% chance to gain 1 lucky die with holy aura, 50% chance to gain 1 lucky die with ice aura. No cost — pure positive event.
- **"Quarry Site"** — Abandoned quarry with good stone. Take what you can carry: gain 2 stone dice. Spend $5 to hire a crew to load more: gain 2 stone dice with ice aura.
- **"Petrified Forest"** — Beautiful ancient trees turned to stone. Harvest carefully (lose 1 day next round): gain 2 stone dice with holy aura. Blast with dynamite (sacrifice 1 equipment): gain 3 stone dice with fire aura.

### Demon Hunter Exclusive Events (Isaac Granger only)

These supernatural events replace a percentage of the normal event pool when playing as the Demon Hunter. They are more extreme in both directions — devastating consequences or incredible rewards. Target ~30% of events pulled from this pool when playing Isaac.

- **"Crossroads Deal"** — A well-dressed stranger offers a bargain. Accept: gain 1 diamond die with fire aura and red bullet pip sticker, but permanently lose 1 equipment slot for the rest of the run. Refuse: gain $5 and a bone die (he respects your resolve).
- **"Possessed Wagon"** — Your wagon is haunted. Exorcise it (sacrifice a medicine card or Priest's Blessing): gain 1 random equipment with ghost aura and holy aura. Abandon it: lose ALL supply cards and 1 day next round, but gain 2 steel dice with fire aura.
- **"Fallen Angel"** — A wounded celestial being on the trail. Help it (pay $10 and 1 day next round): gain 1 gold die with holy aura and blue moon pip sticker, plus a random equipment with holy aura. Ignore it: nothing happens. Betray it (sacrifice 1 equipment): gain 2 diamond dice with fire aura, but lose all rerolls next round.
- **"Demon's Bounty"** — You slay a lesser demon. Collect its remains: gain 1 steel die with fire aura and red bullet pip sticker, plus $8 bounty. Purify the remains (use a medicine card): gain 1 steel die with holy aura and golden dollar sign pip sticker instead.
- **"Blood Moon Rising"** — All your dice are cursed for next round (scored dice have 25% chance of being destroyed after scoring). Endure it and gain 2 lucky dice with fire aura and purple flower pip stickers at end of round. Or pay $12 to perform a ritual and skip the curse entirely (gain nothing).
- **"The Preacher's Ghost"** — A spectral preacher appears and blesses your journey. Gain 1 random equipment with ghost aura. If you already have Priest's Blessing in inventory, also gain 1 bone die with holy aura and blue moon pip sticker.
- **"Hellmouth"** — A crack in the earth belches fire. Descend (lose 2 days next round, sacrifice 2 random dice): gain 1 diamond die with fire aura and red bullet pip sticker, plus 1 gold die with fire aura. Seal it (sacrifice 1 equipment): gain 3 stone dice with holy aura. Walk away: lose 1 day next round from detour.
- **"Cursed Graveyard"** — Unholy ground radiates power. Desecrate it: gain 3 bone dice with fire aura and red bullet pip stickers, but next boss is upgraded x2 (doubled base score). Consecrate it (sacrifice a medicine card or $8): gain 2 bone dice with holy aura and golden dollar sign pip stickers. Pass through carefully: 50% nothing, 50% lose 1 random enhanced die.
- **"Angel's Armory"** — Hidden cache of holy weapons. Gain 1 random rare equipment with holy aura. Costs $15 to unlock. If you can't afford it, gain 1 steel die with holy aura as a consolation.
- **"Demonic Duel"** — A demon challenges you directly. Fight (sacrifice your most expensive equipment): gain 2 diamond dice with fire aura and red bullet pip stickers, plus $15 bounty, plus a Frontier Encounter card. Flee: lose $10 and 1 day next round. Bargain: lose 3 random enhanced dice, gain 1 equipment with ghost aura (random rare).
- **"The Witch's Offer"** — She offers to enchant your dice. Accept: choose 3 dice from your collection — each gains fire aura. Decline: she curses you — lose 1 reroll next round. Burn her at the stake (sacrifice 1 equipment): gain 2 lucky dice with holy aura and purple flower pip stickers.
- **"Infernal Forge"** — A demonic blacksmith offers his services. Sacrifice 3 dice of any type: gain 1 die of the same base type with fire aura, red bullet pip sticker. Or sacrifice 1 equipment: gain 2 steel dice with fire aura.
- **"The Harrowing"** — Demons assault the wagon train all night. Lose 2 days next round AND -1 hand size next round (exhaustion). At the end of the ordeal, gain: 1 random uncommon equipment with ghost aura, 2 bone dice with fire aura and red bullet pip stickers, and $10. The worst penalty but the best total haul — if you can survive the next round.

### Uneventful Events (No mechanical effect — just flavor and a breather)

- **"Clear Skies"** — Blue sky stretches to the horizon. A perfect day on the trail.
- **"Beautiful Sunset"** — The wagon train stops early to watch the sun dip behind the mountains.
- **"Campfire Songs"** — The group gathers around the fire and shares songs from back home.
- **"Deer Spotted"** — A buck watches from a ridge, then vanishes into the pines.
- **"Calm River"** — A gentle stream runs alongside the trail. Good water, easy crossing.
- **"Wildflowers"** — The prairie is carpeted in color for miles. Morale is high.
- **"Cool Breeze"** — A welcome wind rolls through after days of heat. Everyone breathes easier.
- **"Starry Night"** — Thousands of stars overhead. The children try to count them.
- **"Morning Frost"** — A thin layer of ice on the grass catches the dawn light. Beautiful and brief.
- **"Eagles Overhead"** — A pair of bald eagles circle above the wagon train. Good omen.

---

**Why it works:** Adds narrative variety, tough decisions, and makes each run feel like a *story*. Deeply thematic. Also breaks up the rhythm of play→shop→play→shop.

**Design notes:**
- Most negative events offer a "pay money" or "sacrifice resource" escape hatch — this makes money management more interesting since you're not just saving for the shop
- Events that steal days are the scariest because days = scoring opportunities. These should be rarer or more expensive to avoid
- Professions naturally create different event strategies (Banker can pay through anything, Farmer's extra rerolls absorb reroll-penalty events, Hunter/Scout could get unique event resolutions)
- Consider showing event *type* icon before revealing details if you add the Spyglass equipment — lets players prepare mentally without full spoilers
